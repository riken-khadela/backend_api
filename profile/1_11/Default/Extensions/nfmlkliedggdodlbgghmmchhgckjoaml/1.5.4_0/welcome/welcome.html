Blockify
v1.5.4

Say goodbye to embarrassing Spotify ads!


Please note:

Due to its default behavior, Spotify disables some buttons (like Next / Previous) while playing an advertisement.
When Blockify removes an advertisement, it sometimes may require 5 to 10 seconds to successfully re-enable those buttons.


  • How to use:
  • Ad-blocking is automatically applied as soon as you visit open.spotify.com
  • Use Alt + S shortcut to seamlessly open Spotify web, anytime & anywhere
  • Alternatively, you can simply click on the Blockify extension icon to open Spotify.
    Don't forget to pin Blockify in your browser extension menu/toolbar.


Shortcuts not working?
The shortcut keys may not work if the key combination is already being used by another extension.
To re-allocate your shortcut combination keys, click here.


  1. Open Chrome Shortcuts Manager- chrome://extensions/shortcuts
  2. Find & scroll to "Blockify"
  3. Click on the edit button to change the key combination for opening Spotify web
  4. Click on the input area & press Alt + S to re-assign & save the shortcut
